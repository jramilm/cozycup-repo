About Us

In 2020, I had an idea during the pandemic. I realized that the pandemic brought immense stress to the people. This lead to the idea of building a place where people could relax, enjoying the ambiance of the environment while cozying for a cup of coffee.

We decided to open CozyCup Cafe, focusing on crafting unique flavors and creating a welcoming space. After finding an old building, they worked hard to transform it. Jake curated a menu of sandwiches, pastries, and quality coffee, while I handled the cozy decor.
